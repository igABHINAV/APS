  • We
    believe in
    passionate people


    We aspire, grow, and enhance—the essence of APS at IIITDM Jabalpur. United under the cosmic canopy, we explore the universe's mysteries with passion. Through telescope nights and James Webb workshops, we invite minds to marvel at the night sky, reminding us that our potential, like the cosmos, knows no bounds. APS is more than a club; we're a community of dreamers and achievers, bound by the belief that the pursuit of knowledge is an endless journey. Together, we rise above challenges, reaching for the stars and inspiring others. Let's continue to aspire, grow, and enhance—actively shaping our cosmic narrative and leaving a legacy of discovery.
